# BackRoad — The Product

BackRoad is a social media and automation platform where YOUR data works for YOU.

## What it does

You connect your stuff:
- Your AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, whatever)
- Your smart home devices (the ones that currently tell Amazon before telling you)
- Your social feeds (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube)
- Your abandoned projects (that Vercel page, that MCP server, that app you never launched)
- Your tools, your APIs, your agents

BackRoad tunnels it all through BlackRoad infrastructure and creates AI agents that:
- Automate your social feeds (post, engage, grow — authentically)
- Monitor your home (and tell YOU first, not some corporation)
- Launch your abandoned projects (we help you ship)
- Monetize your content (people like it? you get paid)
- Monetize your tools (that API nobody uses? someone will)
- Turn your life's data into agents that work FOR you

## Revenue model

**We take 10%. The rest is yours.**

- You create content people like → you get paid, we take 10%
- You build a tool people use → you get paid, we take 10%  
- Your agent helps someone → you get paid, we take 10%
- Your data trains a model that serves others → you get paid, we take 10%

## Why this works

Everyone has:
- Smart devices reporting to corporations, not to them
- Social media feeds they don't control
- Side projects they never launched
- Data scattered across 100 services
- AI subscriptions they barely use

BackRoad says: bring all of it here. We'll wire it together. Your AI providers power your agents. Your data fuels your feed. Your abandoned projects become live products. Your smart home reports to YOU.

We're the underdog on top. The one that cares. The one that says your data — all of it, your whole life — should work for you, not against you.
